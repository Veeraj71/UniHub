General Questions

What is UniHub?
UniHub is a comprehensive platform designed to help university students connect, collaborate, and access resources. It serves as a centralized hub for academic discussions, resource sharing, event planning, and campus community building.
Who can use UniHub?
UniHub is primarily designed for university students, professors, and staff. Anyone with a valid university email address can create an account and access the platform's features.
Is UniHub free to use?
Yes, UniHub is completely free for all university students and staff members. We believe in providing equal access to educational resources and networking opportunities.
How do I create an account?
You can create an account by clicking the "Sign Up" button on the homepage and following these steps:
  1. Enter your university email address
  2. Create a secure password
  3. Fill in your basic profile information
  4. Verify your email address through the confirmation link sent to you
Which universities are supported?
UniHub currently supports a growing number of universities. Check our university list on the registration page to see if your institution is included. If your university isn't listed, you can submit a request for inclusion.

Features and Functionality

What features does UniHub offer?
UniHub offers several key features:
  • Study Groups: Create or join study groups for specific courses
  • Resource Library: Access and share notes, textbooks, and study materials
  • Discussion Forums: Engage in academic discussions and seek help
  • Event Calendar: Keep track of campus events and academic deadlines
  • Marketplace: Buy and sell textbooks, supplies, and other student essentials
  • Job Board: Find internships, part-time jobs, and career opportunities
How do I join a study group?
You can join a study group by browsing the "Study Groups" section, filtering by your course or interest area, and clicking "Join Group." Some groups may require moderator approval before you can join.
Can I create my own study group?
Yes! Click on "Study Groups" in the navigation menu, then select "Create New Group." Fill in the group details, set privacy preferences, and invite classmates to join.
How do I upload resources to share?
Navigate to the "Resource Library," click "Upload," select your file, add relevant tags and course information, and choose sharing permissions. Your resource will be available based on your selected visibility settings.

Privacy and Security

How is my personal information protected?
UniHub takes your privacy seriously. We use industry-standard encryption protocols to secure your data. Your personal information is never shared with third parties without your explicit consent. For more details, please refer to our Privacy Policy.
Can anyone see my profile and posts?
You have full control over your privacy settings. By default, your profile is visible to other verified university users, but you can adjust these settings in your account preferences to limit visibility as needed.
Are my study materials protected by copyright?
When uploading materials, please ensure you have the right to share them. UniHub respects intellectual property rights and has systems in place to address copyright violations. Users should not upload copyrighted materials without proper authorization.

Technical Support

What devices and browsers are supported?
UniHub is optimized for use on desktop and mobile devices. We support the latest versions of Chrome, Firefox, Safari, and Edge browsers. Our mobile app is available for both iOS and Android devices.
I'm experiencing technical issues. How can I get help?
If you encounter any technical problems, please visit our Help Center or contact our support team at support@unihub.com. You can also use the "Report Issue" feature within the platform to provide details about the problem you're experiencing.
How do I reset my password?
Click on the "Forgot Password" link on the login page. Enter your registered email address, and we'll send you instructions to reset your password. For security reasons, password reset links expire after 24 hours.

Community Guidelines

What are the rules for using UniHub?
UniHub is committed to maintaining a respectful and productive environment. Users are expected to:
  • Respect other users' privacy and intellectual property
  • Avoid posting inappropriate or offensive content
  • Refrain from spamming or soliciting
  • Use the platform primarily for academic and university-related purposes
What happens if someone violates the community guidelines?
Violations of our community guidelines may result in content removal, temporary suspension, or permanent account deactivation, depending on the severity and frequency of the violation. Users can report inappropriate content using the "Report" feature.
How can I report inappropriate content or behavior?
If you encounter content that violates our community guidelines, click the "Report" button next to the content. Fill out the report form with relevant details, and our moderation team will review your submission promptly.

Account Management

How do I update my profile information?
Navigate to your profile by clicking on your username in the top navigation bar. Select "Edit Profile" to update your information, change your profile picture, or adjust your privacy settings.
Can I connect my UniHub account to other social media platforms?
Yes, you can link your UniHub account to other platforms for easier sharing and networking. Go to "Account Settings," select "Connected Accounts," and follow the instructions to link your desired platforms.
How do I delete my account?
If you wish to delete your account, go to "Account Settings," scroll to the bottom, and click "Delete Account." Follow the prompts to confirm your decision. Please note that account deletion is permanent and cannot be undone.

Getting Involved

How can I become a moderator for a forum or study group?
Forum and study group creators can assign moderator roles to trusted members. If you're interested in moderating, reach out to the current administrator or creator of the group. For platform-wide moderation opportunities, check our "Get Involved" page.
Does UniHub have ambassador programs for universities?
Yes! Our Campus Ambassador Program allows enthusiastic students to represent UniHub at their universities. Ambassadors help promote the platform, organize events, and provide feedback. Visit the "Campus Ambassadors" page to learn more and apply.
How can I provide feedback or suggest new features?
We value your input! Use the "Feedback" button in the footer of any page to share your thoughts, report issues, or suggest new features. Our development team regularly reviews user feedback to improve the platform.
